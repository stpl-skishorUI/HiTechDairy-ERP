Vehicle Tracking System

The Fleet Express solution helps to gain visibility into fleets with live vehicle tracking and real-time data. Route optimization, performance analytics, and engine diagnostics help streamline the operations with real-time vehicle tracking and monitoring, and performance data provides insights into the fleet's route runs, trip histories, fuel usage, and time on site.

  • implementation of a GPS-enabled VTS (vehicle tracking system).
  • tracking of vehicles in a centralised system
  • able to check the travel maps of vehicles with travel histories.
  • Keep watch over the speed of vehicles.
  • Get notifications and alerts on breakdowns.
  • Tracking the vehicle location using the VTS Dashboard
  • web-based or mobile app (Android, iOS)-based vehicle tracking system.
