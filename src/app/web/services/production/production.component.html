Production

The Hitech Dairy ERP Production module is interlinked with the Procurement module, the milk that is procured will be sent to the plant for further processing. Production processes cover the entire range of milk and milk products, with configurable parameters to achieve material balancing across the production processes.

  • Inward material data.
  • Create template.
  • Create batch.
  • Quality control test online.
  • Carry out aggregate material balancing.
  • Define products with varying active ingredients and their percentage.
