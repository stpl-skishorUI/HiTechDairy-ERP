Field Force Tracking

Managing the entire sales team, field force staff, procurement team, including the dairy supervisor, route manager, and veterinary doctors, can be a hassle if the dairy deals with multiple vendors, outlets, distributors, and retailers spread across a location. But when the Field Force Tracking module is integrated with the app, companies can keep track of every activity performed by on-ground executives and the sales team.

  • real-time visibility. Monitoring the live tracking screen for each department's field employees
  • Bulk Task Upload
  • mobile application and SOS button
  • Reminders and Alerts
  • Reports on assignments, targets, visits, and travel routes
