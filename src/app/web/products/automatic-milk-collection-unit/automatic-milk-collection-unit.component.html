Automatic Milk Collection Unit

It is a desktop-based, mobile-based, web-based, and DPU-based automatic milk collection unit that provides a comprehensive solution to perform operations like milk quality testing and weighing at the time of milk collection. The AMCU captures the exact quantity, fat, and solids of milk in real-time and automatically calculates payment to the farmer, which enhances transparency, quick milk collection, easy data management, and instant notifications to the farmer.

  • Software that is desktop-based, Web-based, app-based, or DPU-based
  • The list of accounts, advances, cattle feed, deductions, and bonuses are directly connected to accounting.
  • Centralized rate card management
  • If a S.N.F. counting machine is not available, then you can calculate S.N.F. by using C.L.R.
  • Rate cards can be prepared according to FAT and SNF.
  • A number of reports can be generated.
  • Mobile app synchronisation and SMS integration
  • Generates MIS reports.
