e-Anifarm Desktop

When the number of livestock is limited, it is quite easy to keep the record of all animals manually using ledgers. But as the number of animals increases, it becomes very difficult to record and keep track of all the animals individually. Record-keeping becomes very messy. E-Anifarm software helps large-scale dairy farmers maintain records of all the animals using the desktop application. This has the unique features to maintain the cattle farms.

  • Animal Listing
  • Maintain Breeding Cycles
  • Milk production
  • Sale of milk
  • AI, PD, calving details
  • Medical attention and vaccinations
  • Labourer Attendance
