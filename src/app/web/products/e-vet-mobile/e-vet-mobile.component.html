e-Vet Mobile

This application helps to veterinary doctors to check the details of the animals under to the dairy which has the features mentioned below:

  • Farmers and Animals Registration.
  • Record scheduled and daily activities.
  • Animal Medication and Vaccination alerts to farmers
  • Inventory and Expenses Management
  • Animal Breeding reports.
  • Assistance for improvised milk production.
  • Events and notifications alerts.
