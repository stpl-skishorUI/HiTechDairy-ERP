Products

Automatic milk collection unit

It is a desktop-based, mobile-based, web-based, and DPU-based automatic milk collection unit that provides a comprehensive solution to perform operations like milk quality testing and weighing at the time of milk collection. The AMCU captures the exact quantity, fat, and solids of milk in real-time and automatically calculates payment to the farmer, which enhances transparency, quick milk collection, easy data management, and instant notifications to the farmer.

e-Anifarm Mobile App

e- Anifarm is a simple and most user-friendly application developed by Shaurya Technosoft Pvt. Ltd. for the purpose of herd management in dairy farming. For more convenience, this app is also available in regional languages. While using our application farmers won’t be required to carry the big ledgers for keeping the daily records like milk production per day per shift by each animal, respective sale of milk, AI, PD, calving details, medication and vaccination etc. All these details can be stored in our application in systematic manner with ease.

E-Anifarm Desktop Application

When the number of livestock is limited, it is quite easy to keep the record of all animals manually using ledgers. But as the number of animals increases, it becomes very difficult to record and keep track of all the animals individually. Record-keeping becomes very messy. E-Anifarm software helps large-scale dairy farmers maintain records of all the animals using the desktop application. This has the unique features to maintain the cattle farms.
